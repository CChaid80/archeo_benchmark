# Dataset access

The ARKEOCERA dataset is not distributed directly in this repository.

Access to the dataset is limited to research projects. Requests should be sent
to the contact email listed in the main `README.md`.

Please include the following information in your request:

- your name and institutional affiliation;
- the research project or scientific context;
- the intended use of the dataset;
- whether the request concerns reproduction of the benchmark, extension of the
  study, teaching, or another research purpose;
- any planned redistribution or publication involving the data.

Requests are reviewed individually. Access may be refused or limited when the
intended use is outside the scope of research or does not provide sufficient
information about the planned use of the dataset.
